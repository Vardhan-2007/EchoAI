Score = Task Points + Focus Points + Habit Points

1. Task Points — max 40 pts
(completed tasks / total tasks) × 40
Example: 3 done out of 6 tasks → (3/6) × 40 = 20 pts

2. Focus Points — max 40 pts
(total focus hours × 8)  capped at 40
Example: 3 hours focused → 3 × 8 = 24 pts · 5+ hours → full 40 pts

3. Habit Points — max 20 pts
(habits completed today / total habits) × 20
Example: 3 out of 5 habits done today → (3/5) × 20 = 12 pts

Productivity %

Productivity % = (completed tasks / total tasks) × 100

The color also changes based on the value:

🟢 > 70% → green
🟡 > 40% → yellow/amber
🟣 ≤ 40% → purple (accent)
